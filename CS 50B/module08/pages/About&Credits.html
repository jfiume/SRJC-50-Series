About

The inspiration for a website about martial arts is that I have always enjoyed learning martial arts. I also happen to be 1/4 Chinese. When I was a boy, I learned Tai Quan Do. As an adult I learned Bruce Lee's Jeet Kune Do. Now as I get older I am more interested in Thai Chi.
